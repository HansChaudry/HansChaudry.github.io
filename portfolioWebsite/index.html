    Early Life

    Everything began in Port-Au-Prince, Haiti on September 10, 2002. I lived in Haiti for 7 years, and moved to the USA in 2011. Moving here sparked my passion for technology, I saw the amazing things it can help us achieve and I want to be a part of that. The passion grew after i took apart grandma's pc, and learned about Computer Science. 

    Adolescence

    The beginning of my teenage years were occupied by basketball, video games, school, and going to the library(they had a game room and plenty of computers). I began to focus on a career in CS at the age of 16. I started out by participating in the hour of code(code.org). I taught myself my python during the summer of 2019, and the rest is history. The most important lesson I learned during this point in my life was to "not cheat yourself." When you don't put full effort into your goals, you're only hurting yourself.
