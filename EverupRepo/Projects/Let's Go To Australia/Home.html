What can you do in Australia?

The Harbour Bridge Climb is an Australian tourist attraction loacted in Sydney. The Climb take guests to heights of 134 metres above sea level. Since its launch on 1 October 1998, BridgeClimb has welcomed over 4 million people onto the Sydney Harbour Bridge arches. Prices range from $139 - $403.

June to October is the best time to visit the Great Barrier Reef. The temperature hovers in the low 60s to mid-80s, not much rainfall , which means clearer waters and better diving conditions. Lastly, this peak season offers some key advantages for travelers.
