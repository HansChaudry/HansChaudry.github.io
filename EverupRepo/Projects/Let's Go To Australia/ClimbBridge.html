Climb Harbour Bridge


Climb the Sydney's Harbour Bridge to experience views from a height of 440 feet abave sea level. Climbs to the summit are limited to 14 participants to ensure a small group. Choose a half-day BridgeClimb with a guide in the morning, or climb at dawn, twilight, or night.

Fun Facts

  • It took 1400 workers 8 years to complete the bridge
  • It’s the world’s largest steel arch bridge
  • The arch of the bridge changes height
  • Most of its steel came from the other side of the world
  • A worker named Vincent Kelly survived a 55m fall from the bridge
  • Australia didn’t finish paying for the bridge until 1988
  • The bridge held the Olympic Rings during the Sydney 2000 Olympics
